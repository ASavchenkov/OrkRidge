====== New Elfington Street Race ======

Where there's a will, there's a way, and in this case, both exist to import **Dinosaurs** and other strange animals from **Chult** to use in street races.

The contestants tonight are:

  * A 2 thousand kilo Stegosaurus **( S-2000 )**
  * A 7 foot tall Rat **( RX-7 )**
  * A Giant T-Rex **( GT-R )**

A fourth is needed.
